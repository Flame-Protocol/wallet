Common Questions

How do you ensure compliance with SEC and FINRA regulations?
Our custody solution includes comprehensive audit trails, tamper-evident procedures, and documentation packages designed to meet SEC custody rule requirements. We work with your compliance team to ensure alignment with Rules 206(4)-2 and provide attestation reports from independent auditors.
What insurance coverage is provided?
Each implementation includes up to $10M in crime and specie insurance coverage through Lloyd's of London syndicates. Coverage includes employee theft, fraud, and physical loss of key material. Higher coverage limits available for enterprise clients.
Can this integrate with our existing custody platform?
Yes. We provide API access and standardized data formats compatible with major custody platforms including Fidelity Digital Assets, Anchorage, and Fireblocks. Our solution can operate as a standalone service or integrate seamlessly into your existing infrastructure.
What happens during a disaster recovery scenario?
Our business continuity plan includes geographically distributed backup systems, documented recovery procedures with defined RTOs and RPOs, and 24/7 emergency support. All key material follows the 3-2-1 backup rule with encrypted redundancy across multiple secure locations.
How does your solution differ from qualified custodians?
We provide the technical infrastructure that enables your firm to offer qualified custody services. Our air-gapped key generation and secure storage protocols meet the operational requirements while you maintain the custodial relationship with your clients.
What certifications and audits do you maintain?
We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, undergo annual penetration testing by independent security firms, and provide quarterly security attestation reports. All cryptographic implementations are FIPS 140-2 Level 3 compliant.
